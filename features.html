Features of Chrono Engine

  • Powerful Rendering Engine: Chrono Engine uses Direct3D 11 for rendering, ensuring high performance and stunning visuals.
  • Flexible Asset Management System: Manage your game assets efficiently with our flexible asset management system.
  • Intuitive Design Interface: With ImGui for its user interface, Chrono Engine provides a simple and easy-to-use interface for game developers.
  • Support for Direct3D 12 and OpenGL ES3: Chrono Engine supports Direct3D 12 and OpenGL ES3, ensuring compatibility with a wide range of hardware.
